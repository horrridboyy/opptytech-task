Sandwich Preparation

The steps to prepare a delicious sandwich.

  1. Gather all the required ingredients.
  2. Prepare the bread by slicing it.
  3. Spread the desired condiments on the bread.
  4. Add the main filling ingredients.
  5. Place the top slice of bread on top.
  6. Cut the sandwich in half if desired.
  7. Hola,Serve your delicious sandwich!
